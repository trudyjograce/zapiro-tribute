Decadent Dairy Free Ice-Cream

This is a wonderfully easy recipe to try at home. You do not have to be Jaime Oliver inorder to pull this off but you will definitly improve you ranking amidst friends nd family in the hot summer days to come

Dairy free sounds daunting, but if you use the right combinations your dairy-loving frineds wont even know the diffrence. For this recipe Ive used coconiy milk because its thick consistency is similar to that our pouring cream and is complimentary to almost any flavouring you may want to add

PS.You do not need to have an ice-cream maker for this recipe

Ingredients

  • Full Fat Coconut Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract

As optional extras you can add any of the following ingredients

  • cocoa powder
  • berries
  • nuts

Directions

Preparing Your Custard

Heat up the coconut milk, sugar, and salt in a small saucepan, whisking until sugar has dissolved. Cook, stirring frequently, until the ice cream base begins to thicken, fully coating the back of a wooden spoon.Transfer the custard to a shallow container, cover, and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, until the custard is chilled all the way through.

Churn Your Ice-Cream

Add the chilled ice cream base to the frozen ice cream bowl and churn according to manufacturerâ€™s instructions. Church until it becomes thick and resembles soft serve.

Ready to Eat

You can serve now or transfer it to loaf pan and store in the freezer until ready to serve. Allow 5-10 minutes to thaw when removing from freezer.
