Cartooning for Peace is an international network of committed press cartoonists, who fight with humour for the respect of cultures and freedoms. Our network provides visibility and support for those who are unable to work freely or whose freedom is threatened; It uses the educational value of press cartoons amongst young people; It organizes meetings between cartoonists and a large audience and sets up thematic exhibitions showing a critical look on society.
